# About the Creator

## Yanet Poey Lozano, BSN, RN

Registered Nurse | Computer Engineer | Healthcare AI

Yanet Poey Lozano is a Registered Nurse with a background in Computer Engineering and a passion for responsible artificial intelligence in healthcare.

Her professional journey combines technology, clinical education, and direct experience supporting patients and families.

Before entering nursing, Yanet studied Computer Engineering at José Antonio Echeverría Technological University (CUJAE) in Cuba. After immigrating to the United States, she transitioned into healthcare and earned her Bachelor of Science in Nursing.

Through clinical education, patient care experience, and navigating complex healthcare systems as a caregiver, Yanet developed an interest in how AI can reduce information barriers and improve communication.

Her goal is to contribute to healthcare AI solutions that make information more accessible, support healthcare teams, and empower patients and families while maintaining safety, ethics, and human oversight.
