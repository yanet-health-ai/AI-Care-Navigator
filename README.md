# AI Care Navigator

## An AI-Assisted Workflow for Improving Healthcare Communication

Project Brief — Version 1.0

Human-Centered AI Workflow for Healthcare Communication and Care Coordination

Created by:

Yanet Poey Lozano, BSN, RN
Computer Engineer | Registered Nurse | Healthcare AI

Overview

Healthcare information is often complex, fragmented, and difficult for patients and families to navigate.

Families managing complex medical situations may receive information from multiple sources, including:

Physicians
Therapists
Schools
Insurance programs
Community services

Although information is available, many families struggle with organizing documents, understanding medical terminology, preparing questions, and communicating effectively with healthcare teams.

AI Care Navigator is a human-centered AI workflow concept designed to help caregivers and healthcare teams transform complex information into organized, understandable resources while maintaining human oversight.

The Problem

Patients and caregivers frequently experience:

Information overload

Large amounts of medical, educational, and administrative documentation.

Communication challenges

Important information may not be organized before appointments.

Administrative burden

Families spend significant time managing:

Reports
Referrals
Therapy documentation
Insurance requirements
School records
Understanding barriers

Medical language can be difficult for non-medical users to interpret.

The Solution

AI Care Navigator explores how responsible AI can assist with:

1. Document Organization

Helping organize:

Medical evaluations
Therapy reports
Educational documents
Care timelines
2. Information Summarization

Transforming complex documents into structured summaries:

Main findings
Important dates
Recommendations documented by professionals
Follow-up questions
3. Appointment Preparation

Helping families prepare for healthcare conversations:

Current concerns
Recent changes
Questions for providers
Important history
4. Communication Support

Assisting with drafts for:

Healthcare provider messages
School communication
Care coordination notes
Example Workflow
Medical Information
        ↓
Reports + Evaluations + Notes
        ↓
AI-Assisted Organization
        ↓
Structured Summary
        ↓
Questions + Action Items
        ↓
Human Review
        ↓
Healthcare Team Communication

Responsible AI Approach

AI Care Navigator follows a human-in-the-loop model.

AI assists with:

✓ Organization
✓ Summarization
✓ Communication support
✓ Information accessibility

AI does not:

✗ Diagnose
✗ Recommend treatments
✗ Replace clinicians
✗ Make healthcare decisions

Healthcare professionals and caregivers remain responsible for reviewing information and making decisions.

Why This Matters

AI has the potential to make healthcare more understandable and accessible.

The goal is not to replace human relationships in healthcare.

The goal is to help:

Patients feel more informed.
Families feel more prepared.
Healthcare teams communicate more effectively.
Professionals spend less time managing administrative complexity.
